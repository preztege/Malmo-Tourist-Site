Malmö

"Diversity, Meetings, Possibilities"

Why Malmö?

"Blending green parks and sandy beaches with a rich culture, history and modern art, Malmö lets you go your own way — literally. Starting in the bustling commercial centre, it’s just a short walk to picturesque Old Town and Malmöhus Castle. From there, you can easily continue to the modern Western Harbour for a perfect snapshot of the Öresund Bridge and Turning Torso. And since Malmö loves food, drinks and good company, you’re never far from fine dining or a proper Swedish Fika."

Places to visit

Turning Torso

Lilla Varvsgatan 14, 211 15.

Take a towering selfie with the tallest residential building in the Nordic countries in the background. Designed and constructed by the renowned Spanish architect Santiago Calatrava, the twisted tower in the Western Harbour of Malmö is one of Skåne’s most visited landmarks.

The Øresund Bridge

Between Sweden and Denmark.

The Øresund Bridge is an approximately 16 km long road and rail link between Sweden and Denmark. But it is much more than that. The Øresund Bridge has created a region with a population of 3.7 million inhabitants.

Emporia

Hyllie Boulevard 19, 215 32.

Emporia is a shopping mall and one of the biggest ones in Scandinavia. It is situated in the city of Malmö in Sweden, near Malmö Arena and Hyllie railway station.

Moderna Museet

Ola Billgrens plats 2-4, 211 29.

Moderna Museet Malmö is a museum of modern and contemporary art located in Malmö, Sweden. It is a part of the state-owned Moderna Museet but has an independent exhibition programme. The museum was opened in December 2009

Folkets Park

Amiralsgatan 35, 214 37.

Folkets Park in Malmö is the world's oldest folk-movement park.For over 125 years people have come here to have fun, relax and enjoy. The charming park has many sides. It has a fabulous cultural center, a rich dance palace, beautiful outdoor dining, adventure golf, terrarium and a big park square - and lots of activities for children. The park is also lush green - with ponds, lawns and a hugely popular playground, recently rated to Malmö's best. In winter, the large splash dam transforms into an ice skating rink.

Pildammsparken

Pildammsparken, 217 46.

Pildammsparken is a beautiful park full of runners and families taking a walk or doing outdoor activities together. You can go there just to sit on a bench and watch the beautiful lake or to use the free outdoor workout equipment- either way it's the perfect place to spend a sunny Sunday morning.

Malmö Opera

Östra Rönneholmsvägen 20, 211 47.

In Malmö, Malmö Opera is a funky building with one of Europe's biggest scenes and a lounge with 1,511 seats. Here a performance is played virtually every evening; opera, musicals, concerts and dance performances. There is something for all ages and tastes and here everything is played from old and traditional to modern and newly written.

Swedbank Stadium

Eric Perssons väg 31, 217 62.

The Swedbank Stadium, often called Stadion, is a football stadium in Malmö, Sweden and the home of Allsvenskan club Malmö Fotbollförening, commonly known as Malmö FF. Swedbank Stadium is not only a modern football stadium, it is a flexible event facility and a natural venue for sports, culture and business all year round
